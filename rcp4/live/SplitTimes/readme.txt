Requirements:
-------------
The plugin needs at least remoteCP 4.0.3.6 or newer


Plugin:
--------
Displays a Split-Times box inside the SplitTimes-container.
Plugin gets automatically disabled if the gamemode is not TimeAttack or Laps


Install:
--------
1. Copy all folders to your remoteCP-4 /live/ directory
2. Open your /xml/settings/default/live.xml and add inside <plugins></plugins>:

	<plugin>SplitTimes</plugin>

3. Restart your remoteCP4 Live and enjoy :)
